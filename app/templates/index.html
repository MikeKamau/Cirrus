Welcome to Cirrus, a very simple and lightweight cloud filestore that utilizes Flask and Boto3.

The app also implements the following features:

  • User registration
  • Ability to send confirmation emails to registered users
  • Login and logout
  • Password reset
  • Uploading, listing, downloading, deleting and sharing files through S3 pre-signed urls (Users must confirm their email addresses after registration to use this feature)
